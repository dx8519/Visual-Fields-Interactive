Visual Pathways Simulation

Medical students will become very familiar with visual field diagrams during their neurology or ophthalmology rotations. We decided to make an interactive version of these diagrams that runs client-side using three.js.

Double-click on any pathway to simulate a lesion at that point; double click anywhere else to reset; scroll to zoom and drag to rotate.

Made by Jamin Wu and David Xu.
